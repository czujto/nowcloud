## Privacy and Cookies

nowcloud.pl is a personal Azure architecture blog. The site may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage, such as which architecture notes are useful to readers.

Analytics cookies are optional. When the consent notice is shown, you can accept or reject analytics cookies. No advertising cookies are intentionally used.

This site does not currently collect newsletter sign-ups or email addresses.

Links to [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kamil-lygas-5804528/) and [GitHub](https://github.com/czujto) lead to external sites with their own privacy practices.

You can clear browser storage or cookies for this site to reset your analytics consent choice.

This page is provided as plain, factual information about the site and is not a legal policy drafted by a lawyer.
